# Arcane Duels — Remastered card art

This directory contains the optional high-resolution card illustrations selected through **Stile grafico carte → Nuovo**.

Current coverage:

- Fire: 13/13 remastered illustrations, 1254 × 1254 PNG.
- Water: 13/13 remastered illustrations, 1254 × 1254 PNG.
- Air: 13/13 remastered illustrations, 1254 × 1254 PNG.
- Earth: 13/13 remastered illustrations, 1254 × 1254 PNG.
- Death: original decoded illustrations are intentionally retained until its remastered set is produced.

The images contain artwork only. Cost, state, frame and other gameplay information remain presentation-layer elements.

## Fire art direction

The set was generated with the built-in image-generation workflow using each decoded original Fire image as a semantic reference. Shared direction: premium hand-painted fantasy game art; detailed painterly brushwork; modern remaster of an early-2000s fantasy card game; square composition; a large readable subject; volcanic shadows with orange, crimson and gold firelight; no text, numbers, UI, frame, parchment or watermark.

Subject mapping:

1. Fire Spikes — two spiralling fire orbs.
2. Orc — green infantry warrior with sword and rectangular shield.
3. Fire Ritual — pentagram altar and five candles.
4. Fire Wall — horizontal defensive wall of flame.
5. Minotaur — horned warrior with a double-headed axe.
6. Flame Wave — a curling wave made of fire.
7. Salamander — red draconic salamander breathing flame.
8. Inferno — supernatural mushroom-shaped conflagration.
9. Warlord — dark-armoured commander with spear and red standard.
10. Fire Elemental — humanoid living flame with a white-hot core.
11. Armageddon — incandescent meteors over a shattered world.
12. Dragon — red dragon with broad wings and a golden underbelly.
13. Efreet — red horned fire djinn with gold bracers and a flame body.
