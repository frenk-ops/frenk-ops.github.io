# Matrice delle specializzazioni originali

I gruppi sono sostitutivi per lega.

## Necromancer

- **Starting:** Deathcraft; Hell Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Death Mystery; Souldrinker; Hell Knowledge
- **Major:** Death Lord; Death Mystery; Souldrinker; Hell Knowledge; Fire Mystery

## BattleMage

- **Starting:** Firecraft; Efreets Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Fire Mystery; Fire Aura; Efreets Knowledge
- **Major:** Fire Lord; Fire Mystery; Fire Aura; Efreets Knowledge

## Druid

- **Starting:** Earthcraft; Stone Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Earth Mystery; Healing Aura; Stone Knowledge
- **Major:** Earth Lord; Earth Mystery; Healing Aura; Stone Knowledge

## ThunderMage

- **Starting:** Aircraft; Titans Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Air Mystery; Faery Master; Titans Knowledge
- **Major:** Air Lord; Air Mystery; Earth Mystery; Faery Master; Titans Knowledge

## StormMage

- **Starting:** Watercraft; Sea Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Water Mystery; Astral Nets; Sea Knowledge
- **Major:** Water Lord; Water Mystery; Astral Nets; Sea Knowledge; Water Penalty

## Wizard

- **Starting:** Elemental Knowledge
- **Advanced:** Elemental Knowledge; Meditation
- **Major:** Elemental Knowledge; Ancient Knowledge; Life Knowledge

## Osservazioni

- Il Wizard Major non possiede Meditation.
- ThunderMage Major riceve anche Earth Mystery.
- Necromancer Major riceve anche Fire Mystery.
- StormMage Major accetta Water Penalty in cambio di Water Lord e del resto del loadout.
